
Kabo Auto Invest v1.0 - Offline Investment Simulator

How to use:
1. Unzip the files to a folder.
2. Open index.html in your browser (Chrome recommended).
3. Click "Simulate Next Day" to simulate daily profits and bonuses.
4. After 365 days simulated, you can click "Withdraw" to simulate withdrawal.
5. Your data is saved locally in the browser storage.

Note:
- This is an offline simulation.
- Investment amount compounds daily with a 5% rate plus bonuses.
- Withdrawal allowed only after full 365-day period.
- Levels upgrade automatically based on profit milestones.

Enjoy testing your investment growth!
